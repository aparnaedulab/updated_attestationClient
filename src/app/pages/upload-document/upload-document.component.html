Upload Marksheets

Upload Marksheet Files

Instructions

1. File size should not exceed 5MB (per document) and accepted formats are jpg, jpeg, png or pdf.
2. Multiple degree marksheet attestation can also be requested (It shall be considered as a single application only)
3. Upload digital copies or legible scanned copies (CamScanner documents will not be accepted)
4. Do not merge marksheets otherwise application will be rejected
5. Incorrect selection of college name will lead to rejection of application
6.ATKT /Fail marksheet should be uploaded in Add More section
7. For (10+3) Diploma holders, No need to upload first year / first two semesters documents.
8.For Currently studying students, No need to upload last year / last two semesters documents.
9. If you select NameChangeLetter option,kindly upload the latest marksheet.
